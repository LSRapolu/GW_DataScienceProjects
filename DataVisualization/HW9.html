Charting the Shift: U.S. Music Industry's Journey from Physical to Digital(1973-2023)

Decoding the Decline of Physical and Rise of Digital Music Formats Through Revenue Trends : This graph highlights the U.S. music industry's shift from physical formats (CDs, cassettes, and vinyl) to digital (streaming and downloads), capturing the decline of traditional sales and the rise of digital platforms. It underscores a transformative period where digital consumption becomes the dominant mode, reshaping how music is accessed and enjoyed.

Note: Music Industry Revenues (adjusted for inflation) by medium, in millions of dollars.

Source: RIAA
